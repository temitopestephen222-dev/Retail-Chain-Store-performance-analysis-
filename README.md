# Retail Chain Performance Analysis — Stakeholder Brief

## Overview
A structured analytical brief prepared for a CEO-level audience, 
investigating the causes of declining performance across a 
30-store retail chain over an 18-month period.

## Business Context
The retail chain provided a dataset of ~50,000 transaction records 
covering store performance, product sales, customer transactions, 
and loyalty programme activity.

## Objectives
- Identify which stores are underperforming and closure candidates
- Determine which products to prioritise across the store network
- Pinpoint high-value lapsed customers for targeted win-back campaigns

## Key Questions Investigated
1. Which stores show the lowest and most consistently declining 
   revenue per transaction?
2. Which product categories drive revenue in strong stores but 
   underperform in weak ones?
3. Which formerly loyal customers have gone silent in the last 
   90 days — and what revenue is at risk?
